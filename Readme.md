# 🌟 Cosmofrancais Institute of French

**Website for Grades 6–10 School Coaching & DELF A1–B2 Preparation**

---

## 🎯 About Us
Cosmofrancais is a French language institute dedicated to helping young learners (grades 6–10) and adults achieve fluency in French. We specialize in:

- **School coaching:** French curriculum support for grades 6–10  
- **DELF Preparation:** A1 to B2 levels  

Our teaching combines fun, interactive lessons with structured exam preparation.

---

## 🏫 Courses Offered
1. **School Coaching (Grades 6–10)**  
   - Grammar, vocabulary, and writing practice  
   - Homework help and exam preparation  

2. **DELF Exam Preparation (A1–B2)**  
   - Listening, reading, writing, and speaking modules  
   - Mock tests and personalized feedback
